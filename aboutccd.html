About IITG Placement Cell

IITG Placement Cell was established in 1996 with an aim to help all the students of the institute find a suitable place for themselves in the real world where they can innovate. Today, the cell has more than 100 members and coordinators to help students achieve their dreams and get into the top firms.

Centre for Career Development (CCD) of the Institute centrally handles all aspects of campus placements for the graduating students of all Departments. CCD is well equipped with excellent infrastructure to support each and every stage of the placement processes. CCD staff members assist in arranging Pre-Placement Talks, Written Tests, Group Discussions, and Interviews etc. are made as per the requirements of the Organizations.
